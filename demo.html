About

PINCODE is an idea born from ambition, shaped by experience, and driven by vision. We are a new-age real estate development firm that believes in creating spaces that stand the test of time — in design, in value, and in experience. With a keen eye for location, a deep commitment to quality, and a philosophy of meaningful design, we’re here to change the way people perceive commercial and lifestyle spaces.

Mr. Gurpreet Singh Wason

Emphasizes his journey from managing factories to leading large-scale real estate projects. He highlights the importance of strong project management, cross-departmental synergy, proactive problem-solving, and humility in leadership. His strategic choice of NOIDA Sector 62 for Capitol Avenue is driven by its connectivity and thriving business ecosystem.

Mr. Manpreet Singh Wason

Focuses on streamlining collaborations and ensuring efficient project execution. He underlines the value of integrated teamwork, rapid yet quality-driven development, and a commitment to social responsibility and sustainable growth. He attributes Maasters’ (and now PINCODE’s) success to principles of transparency, quality, and ethical leadership.

Capitol Avenue

Features

Prime Location

Situated in the heart of Noida’s thriving business district, Capitol Avenue offers unrivaled accessibility to major transportation hubs, corporate centers, and premium neighborhoods. Its strategic location enhances connectivity, supports high footfall, and positions businesses for sustainable growth and success remarkably.

Modern Design

Capitol Avenue showcases a modern architectural design that seamlessly blends functionality with aesthetic appeal. The thoughtfully planned layout, cutting-edge infrastructure, and premium finishes create inspiring workspaces and retail environments, setting new benchmarks for innovation and contemporary elegance for lasting impact.

State-of-the-Art Amenities

Offering a comprehensive range of state-of-the-art amenities, Capitol Avenue ensures a superior working and shopping experience. Facilities include advanced security, high-speed elevators, luxurious lobbies, and efficient infrastructure, all designed to promote comfort, productivity, and a vibrant urban lifestyle with excellence.

Integrated Commercial Ecosystem

Capitol Avenue establishes an integrated commercial ecosystem by uniting office spaces, high-street retail zones, and recreational areas. This holistic environment fosters collaboration, drives business synergy, and creates a dynamic platform for growth, making it an iconic landmark for visionary enterprises.
