About
Pincode Infra

Pincode Infra is an idea born from ambition, shaped by experience, and driven by vision. We are a new-age real estate development company that believes in creating spaces that stand the test of time; in design, in value, and in experience. With a keen eye for location, a deep commitment to quality, and a philosophy of meaningful design, we’re here to change the way people perceive commercial and lifestyle spaces.

Mr. Gurpreet Singh Wason

Emphasizes his journey from managing factories to leading large-scale real estate projects. He highlights the importance of strong project management, cross-departmental synergy, proactive problem-solving, and humility in leadership. His strategic choice of NOIDA Sector 62 for Capitol Avenue is driven by its connectivity and thriving business ecosystem.

Mr. Manpreet Singh Wason

Focuses on streamlining collaborations and ensuring efficient project execution. Vision

We aspire to create timeless legacies with every project, cultivating trust and admiration among our stakeholders while transforming urban landscapes and enriching communities one visionary development at a time with passion and unwavering commitment.

Key Advantages of Our Ayodhya Vision

Direct Airport Access

Located minutes from Maharishi Valmiki International Airport, our project offers unmatched connectivity for tourism, hospitality, and second-home investment in one of India’s most sacred cities.

Faith-Driven Footfall

With year-round pilgrim activity and spiritual events, Ayodhya ensures consistent visitor traffic. Creating a stable rental and hospitality market, unlike any seasonal destination.

Ring Road Advantage

The upcoming 67 km ring road unlocks fresh plotting and warehousing markets. Our project’s positioning along this corridor offers capital appreciation through logistics-backed growth.

Cultural Capital of India

Situated close to heritage paths like Ram Path and Janm Path, our development offers access to a spiritually and culturally enriched ecosystem that enhances value and experience.
